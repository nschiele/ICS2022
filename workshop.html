ICS 2023: International Conference on Supercomputing

June 21-23, Orlando FL

In Conjunction with the Federated Computing Research Conference

ACM International Conference on Supercomputing (ICS) is the premier international forum for the presentation of research results in high-performance computing systems. The 36th conference (ICS-2023) will be held in Orlando, FL.

Workshop and Tutorial Information

  • Workshop on Best Practices and Tools for Computational and Data-Intensive Research
  • Tutorial 1: Using the SPEC HPG Benchmarks for Better Analysis and Evaluation of Current and Future HPC Systems
  • Tutorial 2: OpenPOWER and AI
  • Tutorial 3: Developing HPC accelerators using SDAccel for Xilinx FPGAs on-premise or in-cloud
Unless indicated otherwise in the workshop/tutorial descriptions, the times are 8 am - 11 am
